\f0\b\fs24 \cf0 \ul \ulc0 Carrot soup\
\

\f1\b0 \ulnone A warming dish in the cold.\
\
1 kg 			carrots\
0.5 kg 		parsnip\
2 Deciliter 		yellow onion\
1-2			Garlic\
1.5 L 		vegetable broth (water + vegetable broth)\
3 tablespoons 	creme fraiche\
2 tablespoons	tomato puree\
\
\
1. Fry half of the carrots together with the onions, half of the parsnip, and the garlic together with some oil. When it has turned soft, add tomato puree and fry for one to two minutes more. \
2. Add the vegetable broth, maybe your own, or bought. Let boil until the vegetables have turned soft. Mix with a stick mixer. \
3. Add the rest of the vegetables, and boil until you think they have a nice chewing resistance. \
4. Finish up with the creme fraiche\
\
Serve together with a nice bread and enjoy!. \
