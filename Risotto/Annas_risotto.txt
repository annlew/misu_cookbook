Anna's risotto

Ingredients
1 onion
3 dl rice
1 stock cube
white wine
olive oil

Instructions
Chop and fry onion on olive oil.
When onion is soft, add rice and let fry until it looks transperent.
Add wine and let evaporate.
Add boiling water and stock cube.
Let simmer and go and do something else until rice is burnt (you will know by the sound and smell).
Add more water and stir. 
Repeat the two previous points until rice is soft.
Eat.
